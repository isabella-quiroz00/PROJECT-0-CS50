NEUROSCIENCE

Neuroscience encompasses all the studies and investigations that involve the structure of human’s brain and its connection with behaviors or complex activities that human beings relate with every day. That’s why it’s important not only to get to know brain’s functionality and utility, but also, how information travels throughout the nervous system and generates, as a result of many huge processes, all the different states of mind that humanity knows of.

This web page will provide you general information about some human behaviors such as sleeping, eating and thinking, by explaining all the neural processes that take place in the brain. Also, each section provides new studies, scientific articles and, in overall, new advances in the area of investigation that might be of your interest. Finally, due to the actual global crisis with COVID-19 pandemic, you can learn about how viruses travel all the way from the lungs into the nervous system and generate neurological symptoms in the infected patient.
